How to contact Sowena Beauty Global for wholesale aesthetic product support

A successful aesthetic product enquiry starts with clear communication. Sowena Beauty Global gives clinics, distributors and wholesale buyers a direct way to discuss product categories, request quotation support and clarify sourcing needs through official contact channels.

The fastest way to begin is through WhatsApp. Buyers can share the product names, categories, preferred brands, quantity direction and destination market so the Sowena team can better understand the enquiry. This approach makes communication more efficient than sending scattered product questions across multiple channels.

For general business communication, Sowena Beauty Global also provides an official support email. Partners can use email for company introductions, catalogue requests, documentation discussions and long-form wholesale enquiries. Social channels are available for brand updates and visual product communication.

Best information to include in your enquiry

  • Product names or categories you are interested in.
  • Estimated quantity or order direction.
  • Your business type: clinic, reseller, distributor or partner.
  • Your destination market or country.
  • Any timeline or product comparison needs.
